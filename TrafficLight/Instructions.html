Welcome to the Traffic Light Game

In this game, you will participate in {{ total_rounds }} rounds. Your goal is to cross the finish line while maximizing your endowment.

How to Play:
  • You start each round with an endowment of 20 points.
  • The endowment decreases by 1 point every second while you wait.
  • The traffic light starts RED and will turn GREEN at a random time (between 10-20 seconds).
Crossing the Street:
  • You can choose to Cross (Move) at any time by pressing the button.
  • Waiting for Green: If you wait until the light turns Green, you automatically cross safely. Your score is your remaining endowment.
  • Moving on Red: You can choose to cross while the light is Red to save time (and points).
